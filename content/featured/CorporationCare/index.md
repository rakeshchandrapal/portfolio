Developed JCC and JCC Admin apps for Jamnagar Municipal Corporation during SSIP Gujarat Hackathon 2023 (Sept - Oct 2023).

JCC enables public complaint registration and tracking, while JCC Admin allows corporation employees to manage complaint statuses. Utilized Flutter and BLoC for responsive UI and efficient state management. Employed Firebase for database management and OneSignal for push notifications. Advanced to finals, showcasing innovation in civic technology solutions.
